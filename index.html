Masonite Cook Book

Publicly available cook book/resource allowing users to share their favorite recipes with all visitors. Comes pre-loaded with a number of recipes for all occasions. Simple input process taking a list of both ingredients and instructions/steps and displays them in order and in a aesthetically pleasing manor.

Curensee

Curensee is a collaborative app/website that allows users to track speicifc crypto currencies on an easy to use and visually appealing platform. Supporting full user authentication to ensure each user only views the currencies that they're personally interested in.

BWebSolutions Inventory Tracker

This inventory tracker offers the ability for users to input any items they'd wish to keep track of, from sneakers to clothing and any other item. Users are able to upload information regarding the purchase price and date, as well as an image for easy reference.

Bookmark'd App

Bookmark'd is a collaborative app that is meant to be a subsitute for a browser's native bookmark toolbar. Bookmark'd allows users to keep their important websites accessible throughout all their devices and browsers.

Venting Forum

This is another collaborative project/app. The venting forum is a mini social media site and forum designed for users of all kinds to post rants, raves, and everything between. Users can provide an image along with their message and optionally include information about themselves within the post.

Basketball Trivia

A two player trivia app designed to test user's knowledge on all things NBA and basketball. Users are pitted against one another in a first to ten match of wits.
